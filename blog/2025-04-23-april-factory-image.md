If you're running into problems on a newer build, this older factory image is a known-good fallback. Flash it onto your device, then update to the latest version via OTA from there.

{/* truncate */}

[Download the 20250423 factory image](https://drive.google.com/file/d/18ToqresZP_q5-L5QRDsm1m_bk3nd3sAK)

To flash it, follow the [SP Flash Tool guide](/docs/advanced/flashing/sp-flash-flashing). Once your phone boots back up, head to [Updating your Minimal Phone](/docs/getting-started/updating/updating) to pull the latest update over OTA.
